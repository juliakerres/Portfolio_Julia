all go rhythm

--{to the website}
Today we use digital media as a matter of course, because the online universe has a lot to offer. But hidden behind the visible surface, every click, every like, every comment and every purchase is stored, monitored and used to feed machine learn-ing processes. This technology offers benefits and risks that challenge our society. In my opinion, many users are not familiar with the impact of these algorithms. The campaign all go rhythm aims to address this deficit. In order to raise awareness, I have designed a website presenting validated opinions from scientists in the form of quotes. Animations support each quote and the listed sources offer the reader the opportunity for a conscious discussion.
