Wall Design
Group project
Prof. Dr. Isabel Neagele
As part of a student competition, a fellow student and I develo-ped a wall design for the Dialoguemuseum, which will be reali-sed in the museum in Frankfurtâ€˜s city centre after the planned opening in 2021. The museum gives visitors the opportunity to experience the world of a blind person for a limited period of time and is intended to stimulate discourse on this topic. Visitors are guided by blind or visually impaired people through dark rooms where they encounter various everyday situations. The design is based on our own experiences at the Dialoguemuseum in Trier. Without our sense of sight, it was almost impossible to grasp the size and dimensions of the space through which we were being guided. The designed wallpaper visualises this irritation of spatial perception by depicting a modified spatiality using the principles of the Op Art movement.
